template<typename Kernel>
struct QuantLib::XabrModelTraits< SwaptionVolCubeZabrModel< Kernel > >

Traits specialization for all ZABR model kernels.

Provides 5-parameter support (alpha, beta, nu, rho, gamma) and ZABR-specific interpolation/smile section construction.
